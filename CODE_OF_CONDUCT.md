# SentryLoom Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in SentryLoom a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We will act in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive,
and healthy community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Welcome questions and different experience levels.
- State security claims precisely and correct mistakes openly.
- Respect privacy and never pressure anyone to disclose sensitive incident data.
- Accept responsibility, apologize when appropriate, and learn from feedback.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, trolling, personal
attacks, doxxing, publishing private information, or encouraging unsafe or
malicious use of the project are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report community conduct problems privately to the repository owner through
the contact options on the GitHub profile. Do not use public issues for a
private safety matter. Maintainers may edit, remove, reject, lock, or ban
content or participants when necessary to protect the community.

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone publicly represents the
project.
